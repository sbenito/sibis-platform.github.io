SIBIS

SIBIS is a Scalable Informatics Platform for Biomedical Imaging Studies that focuses on the reuse and integration of of data management systems, analysis tools, and visualization packages from within the Biomedical Informatics community.

Goals

The goal for SIBIS is to provide our users with a platform that automates many operational tasks that are performed as part of biomedical imaging studies, yet also link to emerging standards and linked open data approaches to data exchange and interoperability.

Integrated Tools

  • Data Management Applications: XNAT, REDCap
  • Standards: NIDM
  • Analysis Tools: FreeSurfer, FSL, ANTs
